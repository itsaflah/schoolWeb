Welcome to PPMHSS Kottukkara

Established in 1985, PPMHSS Kottukkara is a beacon of quality education in Kerala. We offer comprehensive higher secondary education in Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams for classes 11 and 12. Our institution is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, strong character, and holistic co-curricular development, preparing students for a bright future.
